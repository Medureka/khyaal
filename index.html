About Us

Welcome to Khayal

A Journey Through Flavors and Ambience. At Khayal, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that transcends borders, tantalizes the taste buds, and immerses you in the vibrant tapestry of Iranian, Turkish, and Lebanese cuisine. Nestled in the heart of Bangalore, our restaurant is a sanctuary of flavors, where every dish is a work of art, and every visit is a celebration of culture and taste..

An Ode to Culinary Heritage:Our restaurant is more than just a place to dine; it's a testament to the rich culinary traditions of Iran, Turkey, and Lebanon. We pay homage to the time-honored recipes, spices, and techniques that have been passed down through generations. Each dish tells a story, and every bite is an homage to the kitchens of these lands.
